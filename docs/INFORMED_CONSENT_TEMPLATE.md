# Informed Consent Form — Template

> **For deployers.** This template accompanies the
> [Participant Information Sheet](PARTICIPANT_INFORMATION_SHEET_TEMPLATE.md)
> and is based on the European Commission's Horizon Europe model consent
> form, aligned with GDPR Articles 6, 7, and 13.
>
> Have it reviewed by your Data Protection Officer and ethics committee.
> Translate as needed: written informed consent under Belgian and EU
> practice must be provided in a language the participant understands.
> Ensure participants receive the Participant Information Sheet **before**
> being asked to sign this form.
>
> Two copies should be signed: one retained by the participant, one
> retained by the institution for the duration required by your
> institutional research-records policy.
